vishnuharshan.com/image-suite
Live Image Suite
✔ Browser-based ✔ Privacy-first ✔ Supports JPG, PNG, WebP ✔ Batch Conversion ✔ Client-side Processing
The Problem

Most online image converters upload your files to a server first — slow, and a privacy risk for anything sensitive. Most people just want to convert a JPG to WebP without handing it to a third party.

The Solution

Handles JPG, PNG, and WebP conversion entirely client-side — nothing uploads. Batch conversion with drag-to-reorder, custom filename patterns, adjustable compression, and paste-from-clipboard.

Technologies

vishnuharshan.com/pdf-suite
Live PDF Suite
✔ Browser-based ✔ Privacy-first ✔ Merge, Split, Image→PDF ✔ Client-side Processing
The Problem

People need to merge, split, or build PDFs without uploading files to a server — most free tools online do exactly that, quietly.

The Solution

Three modes in one tool: merge multiple PDFs, split by page range, and convert images into a PDF with control over page size, orientation, and quality. Nothing uploads — it all runs in the browser.

Technologies
