About

From commerce to code, self-taught.

I enjoy turning ideas into working products.

I'm a BBA graduate from Kerala with no formal tech background — everything I know about building software, I taught myself. That gap became the point: I use AI as a genuine collaborator to design, build, and ship real tools.

My live projects — Image Suite, PDF Suite, and QR Generator — run entirely in the browser. No uploads, no server round-trips, no tracking. I care about tools that respect the person using them.

Outside of building, I'm into cybersecurity and Android malware analysis as a hobby, and I trade forex, crypto, and Indian equities — which is also what pulled me into building TREND, a transparent token project on BNB Smart Chain.
